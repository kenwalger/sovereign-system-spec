docs(spec): codify hydration terminology in emerging field terms

Introduces three core architectural concepts to Section VI of the
Sovereign Systems Specification to formalize read-path context mechanics:
- Context Hydration
- Hydration Boundary
- Hydration Latency

These terms provide an explicit structural metaphor for the boundary transition
where cold, content-addressed ledger blocks are verified out-of-band and
inflated into volatile LLM active session memory.
